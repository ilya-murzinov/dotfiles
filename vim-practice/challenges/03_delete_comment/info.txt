NOTE: f/ lands ON the slash. h moves to the space before it. D deletes space + comment. Clean result with no trailing space.
NOTE: Alternative: T;DZZ (3+1+2=6) — T; goes backward-till-semicolon, landing on space after it, then D. Same count but elegant if cursor is past the semicolon.
