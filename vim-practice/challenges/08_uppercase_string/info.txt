OPTIMAL: 6
SOLUTION_A: gUi"ZZ  —  uppercase operator on inside-quotes text object (g U i " + ZZ = 6 keys)
SOLUTION_B: f"lvt"UZZ  —  find quote, visual select, uppercase (more keys, less idiomatic)
